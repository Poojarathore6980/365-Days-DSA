Approach (Short & Clear)
Sort the array → helps avoid duplicates and use two pointers.
Fix first two numbers (i, j) using nested loops.
Use two pointers (left, right) for remaining two numbers.
Skip duplicates at all levels (i, j, left, right).
Move pointers based on sum comparison with target.

 Time Complexity: O(n^3)
 Space Complexity: O(1) (excluding result list)
